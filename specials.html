July and August:  For the first time in our history, we would like to offer a summer monthly rate for “Cottage Suites”, our two room family apartments.  Rent one of our Suites for the entire month for $1800 + NH tax and use it every day or every weekend.  Rent the suite for both months (July and August) for $3000 + NH tax.  This is an economical way of spending the summer on Squam Lake.  Add the month of September (one of the very nicest months) for an additional $750 + NH tax.  This includes a boat slip on our dock.

Fun Packages

Couples Adventure Package: designed to offer opportunities for partners to improve communication, reconnect in all areas.  Upon checking in, couples will receive four tasks to complete during their stay that will stimulate conversation and creativity!  Two nights including a special gift at the time of check out for only $195 + NH tax.   This is a spin on your typical romantic getaway so consider this for a special celebration gift.
