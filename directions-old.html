directions

We are located off of Interstate 93 at Exit 24; travel south through Ashland to Holderness on Route 3 (also Rt. 25) for approximately 3.5 miles. Cottage Place is on your left. White cottages and main building with cottage suites with green trim - watch for the old red Adirondack chairs!

  • The travel time from Boston is 2 hours.
  • Travel time from Montreal is 3 hours

 

The google map on this page will say the Cottage Place is in Center Harbor, but maps our location correctly in Holderness!

Some mapping systems have us located incorrectly (despite our efforts to correct the problem), so GPS users should enter our coordinates below.
