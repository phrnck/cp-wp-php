Our Cottages, Cottage Suites and Lodge are designed for seasonal comfort and coziness. The individual Cottages range in size from one room, one bedroom to two bedroom each having full kitchens and baths. Several have wood burning fireplaces. The Cottage Suites located in the main building are two room family apartments with full kitchens and baths. The Lodge has six bedrooms, a large living/dining room, a fireplace, a full kitchen and two baths. The Lodge is very popular for special birthday celebrations, anniversaries, weddings, multi-generational family gatherings and has a perfect atmosphere for a business meeting retreat or special interest groups. All rentals have cable TV, wireless internet, outside porches with Adirondack chairs and access to picnic tables and grilles. Other amenities

We are a housekeeping facility, which means our guests enjoy independence and autonomy without the intrusion of daily cleaning service. Our guests arrive to a nicely appointed and well equipped rental with all bath and bed linens provided. Our kitchens are fully equipped with full size appliances, coffee makers and basic kitchenware (pots, pans, dishes, glassware, silverware). Guest guidelines

The lure and romance of the Squam Lakes region offers a great retreat for couples of all ages. The natural setting and convenience to New Hampshire’s best attractions makes us a fabulous spot for a fun family vacation.
