Cottage Suites

The eight Cottage Suites are part of the main building but similar to our independent Cottages. The Cottage Suites all have private entrances at the front of the building and large picture windows overlooking the beach and Evans Cove on Little Squam Lake.

Each Suite consists of two adjoining rooms, a living/dining area and a bedroom, offering a variety of sleeping options including double, queen, and twin beds. All units are air-conditioned and offer cable TV, a kitchen with full size appliances, small kitchenware (pot, dish, silver), a dining area, and a full bathroom with tub and shower.
