reservations

You can check on availability and reserve online, Book Now, or send us some information regarding the dates of your stay, how many people, pets, what you might like to do while you're here and a way to get in touch with you. Include any questions in your note. We will make every effort to respond promptly.

To provide us with the information requested above, check availability and reserve your stay directly with us, or for weekly rates, Email, or call 603 968-7116.

Please include in your email the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Cottage or Suite requested
  • Date of arrival
  • Date of departure
  • Number of guests
  • Pet(s)
  • Boat - length
