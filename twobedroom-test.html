two bedroom cottages

Cottage Two: A living room with a fireplace, full kitchen, dining area, full bathroom, one bedroom has a queen size bed, other bedroom has double bed, twin bed. Nestled on the quiet side of the property!

Accommodates 3-5; Rate: $160 to $235 nightly; $1120 to $1350 weekly.
