The Lodge

The Lodge is perfect for special birthday celebrations, anniversaries, weddings, multi-generational family gatherings and has a great atmosphere for a business meeting retreat. Designed for large families or groups, the Lodge has an oversized living room with a fieldstone fireplace, a well equipped kitchen with full size appliances, two coffee makers, a microwave, and dishware for 15 people. There are large farm style tables for games and meals.

The second floor offers two entirely private bedrooms, and two additional bedrooms which are suites connected by a door. The downstairs offers two more bedrooms, which are also suites connected by a door. There are seven double beds and one twin bed for a maximum capacity of 15 people and there is a bathroom with a shower on each floor. There is a private fire pit and picnic area for our Lodge guests and the large cedar play area is just steps from the outside deck.
