The Cottage Place property offers you a perfect retreat. Relax in one of the many Adirondack chairs on the grounds, sunbath and swim on our private beach, paddle a kayak around Evans Cove, have your lunch on one of the many picnic tables, cook dinner on one of the outdoor grills and then enjoy a camp fire in the evening.
