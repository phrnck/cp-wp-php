amenities

To make your stay more comfortable, we provide all bed and bath linens, basic kitchenware (pots, pans, dishes, glassware, silverware),  cable tv, wireless internet access, gift store, seasonal coffee shop.

We suggest that you bring from home all toiletries, hair dryer, groceries (or buy locally).

We offer the following for your comfort, convenience and enjoyment:

On the Lake:
  • 140 foot sandy beach on Evans Cove, Little Squam Lake
  • Adirondack chairs
  • Large dock available for boat mooring with advance reservations
  • Electrical outlets for boats that need charging
  • Floating raft for swimming fun
  • Kayak Rentals
  • Paddle Boat Rental
On the Property:
  • Large cedar play area for children (www.cedarworks.com)
  • Charcoal grills
  • Communal propane grille
  • Picnic tables
  • Shuffleboard Court
  • Cottage gardens
  • Adirondack chairs and benches
  • Campfire area
  • All rentals have outside porches
Indoors:
  • Kitchens with full size appliances, coffee makers and basic kitchenware (pots, pans, dishes, glassware, silverware)
  • Cable TV
  • Wireless internet
  • Wood burning fireplaces in the Lodge, Cottages 2,3,4 and 6
  • Lobby gift shop with free gift wrapping
  • Seasonal coffee shop - catering available with advance planning
  • Art and craft classes
  • Babysitting service available with advance notice
