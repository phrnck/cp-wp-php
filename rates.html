rates

Our rates vary according to the number of people in your travel party and the time of year you are staying with us. All rates subject to 9% New Hampshire meals and room tax, and payable in US dollars.

You can check availability and Book Now online, or contact us directly to check availability and reserve your stay, or for weekly rates Email or call 603 968-7116.

Call us for competitive long term weekly or monthly rates for an extended stay. These rates apply between Labor Day in September and May 15th. We have had many folks reside with us temporarily for many transitional reasons: building a home in the area, working for an extended period in the Lakes Region, shopping for a home in the area or just enjoying the Holderness area off season. We welcome long term guests - give us a call.
